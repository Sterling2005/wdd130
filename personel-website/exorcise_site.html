The Brittany Spaniel: A Versatile Companion

The Brittany Spaniel, also known as the Epagneul Breton, is a captivating breed originating from France. Renowned for their energetic spirit, intelligence, and exceptional hunting abilities, Brittanys have become cherished companions for active families and outdoor enthusiasts alike.

This website delves into the fascinating world of Brittany Spaniels, exploring their appearance, temperament, care requirements, and rich history. Whether you're a seasoned dog owner or simply captivated by this breed, we invite you to discover the magic of the Brittany Spaniel.

Distinctive Looks: The Brittany's Physical Traits

  • **Medium-sized:** Standing at 17-20 inches tall at the shoulder, Brittanys possess a well-proportioned and athletic build.
  • **Distinctive Coat:** Their dense, medium-length coat comes in various combinations of white, orange, and liver. Feathering adorns their legs, chest, and tail.
  • **Expressive Eyes:** Their intelligent brown eyes radiate alertness and a playful spirit.
  • **Floppy Ears:** Floppy ears with rounded tips frame their expressive faces.
  • **Energetic Tail:** Their high-set tail is often carried proudly, offering clues to their mood and excitement.
